Mingloft Privacy Commitment

At Mingloft, we value and prioritize your privacy. It’s embedded in every layer of our design and development process, ensuring our services and products are ones you can rely on while seeking out shared connections.

Our Promise of Privacy: Every feature and service under the Mingloft banner is crafted with your privacy as a central tenet. Our team collaborates to ensure that privacy is held in the highest regard.

Our Pledge of Clarity: Like you, we navigate various online platforms, and we understand the frustration of complex privacy policies. Our approach is different. We strive for simplicity and clarity in our Privacy Policy, and we genuinely hope you’ll take a moment to understand how we handle your data.

Our Assurance of Security: Our teams work to safeguard your information. We continually refine our security protocols and dedicate resources to ensure that your data remains protected.

Mingloft Privacy Guidelines

Welcome to Mingloft Privacy Guidelines (“Guidelines”)! Here, we illuminate how we manage, store, safeguard, and disseminate your information, and identify the partners we might share it with. For the best understanding, please review this alongside our Terms of Service.

As you navigate through the Mingloft app (inclusive of the desktop variant) (“App”), our associated websites, and our digital offerings (like contests or feedback forms) – collectively referred to in these Guidelines as our “Platforms” – we gather certain user data.

Mingloft operates on a global spectrum. This means your data may traverse and be utilised in regions like the United States and the UK, irrespective of your domicile. This document details how we ensure the safety of your personal data during such transitions.

Who Are We?

Mingloft is the entity behind the App and Platforms. Within this document, we might refer to ourselves as “we” or “us”, encompassing all our associated bodies that play a role in gathering and processing your data via Mingloft.

Our Privacy Guardian is always available for queries or clarifications. Connect with them via email at support@get-Mingloft.com

2. Scope of This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy pertains to websites, applications, events, and other services we facilitate under the Mingloft brand. For clarity, we’ll call all these our services throughout this Privacy Policy. Links to this Privacy Policy are placed across all relevant services for your convenience.

Specific services may necessitate distinct privacy policies. Should a service feature its own policy, that specific policy will take precedence over this general Privacy Policy.

3. Data We Gather

Undoubtedly, to aid you in fostering double dates and shared experiences, we require certain details, like essential profile attributes and preferences regarding potential connections. Information about your interaction with our services, data from third-party platforms, and more also forms part of our collection process. Further elucidation is provided below.

Details You Impart to Us:

  • Account Creation: When registering, you’ll provide at minimum your contact number, email, gender, date of birth.
  • Profile Completion: If you decide, you can disclose your sexual orientation. By doing so, you grant us consent to utilise this data as described in this policy. Optional additions include profile descriptions, hobbies, and multimedia content like images and videos. To add media, permissions to access your camera or gallery might be needed.
  • Payments: For premium services or purchases directly from Mingloft (excluding platforms like iOS or Android), payment information such as debit or credit card details may be required.
  • Feedback: Participating in our feedback mechanisms like surveys and focus groups provides us insights into our service quality.
  • Promotions and Events: Information may be collected if you opt to participate in any promotional events or contests we host.
  • Customer Support: Conversations with our support team are recorded for quality assurance.
  • Shared Details: Any details you might share about others (like friends’ contact details) will be processed to fulfil your request.
  • Interactions: Conversations and content shared with other members are processed to ensure a safe and engaging community environment.

Information Sourced from Third Parties:

  • Members: Users might share data about you as they engage with you through Mingloft.
  • Social Platforms: Linking your social media profiles or importing data from them (e.g., Facebook, Google, Spotify) can provide us with certain details.
  • Partnerships: Information might be relayed from partners, especially concerning promotional campaigns or member safety.

Automatically Generated or Collected Data:

  • Usage Patterns: Interactions with our services provide data about user habits, like the features accessed and how you engage with other members.
  • Cookies and Similar Tools: Technologies like cookies and web beacons are employed to recognise users and their devices. Refer to our Cookie Policy for comprehensive details.
  • Certain browsers have a Do Not Track feature, but due to inconsistencies in its implementation, our services don’t currently respond to such signals.

Data Collected with Consent:

  • Location Data: With your permission, we might gather precise geolocation data. Even when not actively using Mingloft, this data might be collected if you’ve granted persistent access. Denying this access might limit certain geolocation-dependent services.
  • Photo Verification: Engaging in our photo verification process allows us to use facial recognition to confirm your identity. Certain jurisdictions might consider this biometric data.
  • Additional Details: Other data, like multimedia content, might be collected based on specific features or with your explicit consent.

Information We Collect

We collect information both directly from you and sometimes from third parties. Need a deeper dive? Let’s get into the details.

Information You Share with Us:

  • Account Creation: When setting up your Mingloft account, we’ll need your phone number, email, and some basic profile details, like your gender, birthdate, and preferences.

  • Profile Enhancement: You can choose to disclose your sexual orientation, which aids in customising your matches. Anything extra you share here falls under our Privacy Policy.   • Customer Support: When you contact our helpdesk, we record the details of your interaction for better assistance.

  • From Members: Users might provide information about you as they use Mingloft, like when they interact or report an issue involving you.

  • Usage Patterns: As you use Mingloft, we gather insights on how you interact with our features, such as your login times, actions taken, and engagement with other members.

  • Miscellaneous: From time to time, with your consent, we might collect other details, such as photos, videos, or data for new features we’re rolling out.

G. To adhere to legal mandates
