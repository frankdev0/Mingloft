Who We Are

Mingloft isn’t just another event planning tool. With Mingloft, you can visualize and refine every detail of your event—layouts, seating arrangements, and workflows—long before the big day arrives.

Designed to simplify and enhance the planning process, Mingloft brings everyone involved together in one collaborative space. From your team to your vendors, coordination becomes effortless, allowing you to focus on what matters most: creating an unforgettable experience. Turn your vision into reality with ease and confidence, all in one seamless platform

Our Features

Fun Stuff

Mingloft is packed with features designed to enhance your event planning experience, making it fun, organized, and stress-free. Whether you're planning a wedding, party, or corporate event, Mingloft provides the perfect platform to bring your vision to life.

Here’s what you can expect:.

  • Collaborative Planning Tools to seamlessly work with your team and vendors
  • Interactive Event Previews to visualize your layout and seating arrangements before the big day
  • Real-Time Chat & Group Discussions to keep everyone in the loop and make coordination easy
  • Feedback & Poll Tools to inspire collaboration and gather insights from your team and guests.
  • Personalized Event Profiles that showcase your unique style and preferences.
  • Vendor & Guest Management to stay organized and on track with every detail.

Forget the chaos of scattered planning, our group chats let you and your team coordinate every detail, from timelines to vendor tasks.

Add polls, votes, and voice notes to bring ideas to life and ensure everyone is on the same page.
